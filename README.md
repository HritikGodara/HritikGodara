# 💫 About Me:
### Hi there 👋<br><br>🔭 **I'm currently working on** — an NLP pipeline for Indian legal documents: one model that summarizes court judgments into plain language, another that flags risky contract clauses before you sign. Built on `flan-t5-base` and `InLegalBERT`, tested on Kaggle T4x2.<br><br>🤝 **I'm looking to collaborate on** — applied ML projects, especially anything touching NLP, real-world messy data, or Python systems that actually ship. Not interested in toy datasets.<br><br>🤲 **I'm looking for help with** — scaling ML inference and deployment. Training a model is one thing; making it reliable in production is another problem entirely.<br><br>🌱 **I'm currently learning** — the gap between ML research and production systems. Also: why my models perform well on eval sets and quietly fall apart on real inputs.<br><br>💬 **Ask me about** — NLP for low-resource Indian languages, fine-tuning transformer models on domain-specific data, or why your ROUGE score doesn't mean what you think it means.<br><br>⚡ **Fun fact** — I built a clause risk classifier that hits 0.94 macro-F1 and 0.97 precision on high-risk clauses. Law school would have taken longer.
